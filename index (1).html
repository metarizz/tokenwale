This section provides a complete overview of our privacy policy. Click on any topic below to expand it and learn more about how we protect your privacy.

The App is designed to operate without collecting any personally identifiable information (PII) from its users. We do not ask for your name, email address, location, or any other personal details.

Instead of collecting personal information, the App generates a unique, anonymous identifier ("User ID") locally on your device when you first use the App. This User ID is a random string of characters and cannot be used to identify you personally.

This generated User ID is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Internal App Functionality: To distinguish your instance of the App from others, allowing the App to save your progress, settings, or preferences locally on your device.
  • Analytics (Aggregated and Anonymous): If the App uses any analytics tools, they are configured to only record data associated with this anonymous User ID. This data is aggregated and cannot be traced back to you. For example, we might track the total number of times a feature is used, but never who used it.

The generated User ID and any associated data (like local settings or anonymous analytics data) are not transmitted off your device to our servers or any third-party servers. All data related to your use of the App, identified by the generated User ID, remains exclusively on your device.
